Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2024

Welcome to Equ Healthcare. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our website and services. By accessing or using our website, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information from you:

  • Personal Information: We may collect personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, and any other information you provide when signing up for our services, including joining our waitlist for our AI-powered health solutions.
  • Health Information: For certain services, we may collect health-related data such as dietary preferences, physical activity, or medical history, in compliance with applicable healthcare privacy laws.
  • Automatically Collected Information: We may automatically collect data about your device and how you use our website, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, and browsing behavior.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve our AI-powered healthcare solutions, including personalized health plans, activity tracking, and treatment recommendations.
  • Research and Development: To improve our services and develop new health-related AI technologies and solutions.
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries, provide support, send updates, and notify you of important information about our products and services.
  • Compliance: To comply with applicable healthcare regulations and legal obligations, including data protection and patient confidentiality laws.
  • Marketing: With your consent, we may use your information to send you promotional content about our services or new features.

3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with the following parties:

  • Healthcare Providers: If you use our AI solutions in connection with healthcare providers, we may share health-related information as necessary to deliver care.
  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party vendors who assist us in providing our services, such as data storage, analytics, or customer support.
  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations, court orders, or government regulations.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or other business restructuring, your information may be transferred to the new owners as part of the transaction.

4. Your Data Protection Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access and request copies of your personal data.
  • The right to correct or update inaccurate personal information.
  • The right to request deletion of your data, subject to legal requirements.
  • The right to withdraw your consent for processing your information, where applicable.

5. Data Security

We take the security of your personal and health-related information seriously. We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, please note that no method of data transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us improve functionality, analyze website usage, and deliver personalized content. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings, but disabling cookies may affect certain features of our website.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected information from a child, we will take steps to delete that data promptly.

8. International Users

Equ Healthcare is based in the United States. If you are accessing our services from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, processed, and stored in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your country.
